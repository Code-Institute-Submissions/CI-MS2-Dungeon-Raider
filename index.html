Dungeon Raider

Storm the castle, defeat the enemies, claim the treasure!

How to play :

Dungeon Raider is a turn based combat game that has an element of luck involved. Each time someone attacks, the amount of damage and hits vary.

Your mission is to storm the castle, defeat three enemies and claim the treasure.

Moves :

Attack - Attack the enemy with all your might

Counter - Counters the enemy with -25% attack power, but incoming damage is reduced by 50%

Heal - Heals yourself for 250

Stats :

Health - Determines how much health you have

Strength - Determines the strength of your attacks

Agility - Determines the number of hits you are able to do

Speed - Determines who attacks first

Enemies Defeated: 0

Choose your hero :

Arthur
